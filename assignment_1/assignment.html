ABOUT US


At Floral Whispers, we're more than just a flower shop; we're your partners in celebrating life's special moments. With a passion for perfection and a love for all things floral, we dedicate ourselves to creating handcrafted bouquets that embody nature's elegance.


Our journey began with a single bloom, a whisper of nature's beauty, and a desire to share it with the world. Founded by Aruzhan Yermek in 2018, Floral Whispers started as a dream nurtured in the heart of Astana. Over the years, we've grown, learned, and bloomed alongside the flowers we adore.
